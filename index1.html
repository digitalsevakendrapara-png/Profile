Our Services

Quick, efficient, and reliable assistance for all your online documentation and banking needs.

1. e-Governance & Govt Services (G2C)

  • Aadhaar Portal Services: Tracking, biometric/demographic updates (Name, Address, Mobile), and PVC printing.
  • Official Documents: PAN Card, Voter ID, and Passport applications/corrections.
  • e-District Certificates: Income, Caste, Residence, Birth, Death, and Marriage certificates.
  • Ration Card: New registration, member additions/modifications.

2. Central Welfare Schemes

  • Ayushman Bharat: Golden Card registration & printing for healthcare.
  • PM-Kisan: Farmer registration, e-KYC updates, and status checks.
  • Labor & Pension: e-Shram card, PM-SYM, and Atal Pension Yojana (APY) enrollment.
  • Housing & Energy: PM Awas Yojana verification and PM Ujjwala Yojana.

3. Banking & Insurance

  • AePS (DigiPay): Cash withdrawal, deposit, and balance inquiry using biometric thumbprint.
  • Kiosk Banking: Opening Jan Dhan accounts and regular savings portals.
  • Insurance (POS): Life, Health, Motor (Bike/Car), and PM Fasal Bima premium collection.
  • Credit/Loans: Mudra/micro-loan applications and EMI collections.

4. Utility Bills & Recharges

  • BBPS Portal: Instant payment for Electricity, Water, Piped Gas, Landline, and Broadband.
  • Mobile & DTH: Top-ups and prepaid multi-recharges.
  • FASTag Services: Issuance of new FASTags and top-up recharges.

5. Travel & Tourism Booking

  • IRCTC Services: Authorized train ticket bookings, changes, and cancellations.
  • Flights & Buses: Domestic/International flight booking and state/private bus ticketing.

6. Education & Literacy

  • PMGDISHA: Digital literacy training initiatives for rural regions.
  • Student Portals: Admissions/forms for NIOS, IGNOU, and competitive online exam portals.
  • CSC Academy: Computer courses, skill-building programs, and official certifications.

7. Commercial, Business & General Cyber Cafe Works

Taxation Services GST Registration, GST Returns, and Income Tax Return (ITR) filing for individuals or shops.
Business Certifications FSSAI Food License generation and MSME / Udyam registration profiles.
Legal, Health & General Cafe Tele-Legal (lawyer consultation), Tele-Medicine, document scanning, photocopying, lamination, typing, and standard cyber support.
